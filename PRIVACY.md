# Privacy Policy for BarFringer

**Last updated: April 27, 2026**

## Data Collection

BarFringer does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device.

## Permissions

BarFringer requests the following macOS permissions to function:

- **Accessibility**: Required to detect and interact with menu bar items from other applications.
- **Screen Recording**: Required to capture menu bar item icons for display in the Fringer Bar.

These permissions are used exclusively for local menu bar management. No data obtained through these permissions is transmitted off your device.

## Third-Party Services

BarFringer does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

# App Icon — Fringer

## 用途
macOS 应用主图标，显示在 Dock、Launchpad、Finder、关于窗口等位置。

## 需要的尺寸
生成一张 1024x1024 的主图，后续缩放为以下尺寸：
- 1024x1024 (512@2x)
- 512x512
- 256x256 (128@2x)
- 128x128
- 64x64 (32@2x)
- 32x32 (16@2x)
- 16x16

## AI 生成 Prompt

**English:**
A macOS app icon for a menu bar management utility called "Fringer". The icon should convey the concept of organizing and tidying up menu bar icons — like a filter or gatekeeper for the top-right corner of macOS.

Design: A rounded square (macOS icon shape, squircle) with a clean, modern, minimal aesthetic. The main visual element is a stylized top menu bar with several small dots/circles being neatly sorted or filtered — some visible, some fading away or being tucked behind a divider. Use a gradient background transitioning from deep indigo/navy blue to a vibrant purple or teal. The foreground elements (the bar and dots) should be white or light with subtle depth/shadow. The overall feel should be premium, polished, and professional — similar in quality to icons from apps like 1Password, Raycast, or CleanMyMac. No text in the icon.

# DMG Background — 安装拖拽背景图

## 用途
分发 .dmg 安装包时的背景图。用户打开 DMG 后看到的拖拽安装界面（左边 app 图标 → 右边 Applications 文件夹）。

## 技术要求
- **尺寸**: 600x400 像素（标准 DMG 窗口大小）
- **格式**: PNG
- **内容**: 不需要包含 app 图标或 Applications 图标（这些由系统叠加），只需要背景+箭头引导

## AI 生成 Prompt

**English:**
A clean, minimal DMG installer background image for a macOS app, 600x400 pixels. The design should have a subtle gradient background matching the app's brand colors (deep indigo to purple/teal). In the center, a thin elegant arrow pointing from left to right, guiding the user to drag the app to Applications. The arrow should be white or light colored with subtle transparency. The overall feel should be modern and premium. No text, no app icons — just the background and directional arrow. Very clean, almost like frosted glass.

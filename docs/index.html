Take control of your Mac menu bar

A macOS menu bar manager that organizes, hides, and customizes your menu bar icons — so you can focus on what matters.

Features

👁
Hide & Show Menu Bar Items
Instantly hide cluttered icons and reveal them when you need them.
🔍
Quick Search
Find any menu bar item instantly with a fast, keyboard-driven search.
🎨
Custom Presets
Create and switch between different menu bar layouts for work, focus, or play.
⚡
Smart Triggers
Automatically adjust your menu bar based on battery level, Wi-Fi, time, or active app.
⌨
Keyboard Shortcuts
Global hotkeys give you instant access — toggle visibility without touching the mouse.
